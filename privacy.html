Privacy Policy

Effective date: 12 September 2025

App: Autotap
Developer: Apps and Clicks Limited

1. Summary

We do not collect, store, or share personal data via the Autotap app. The app uses Android Accessibility Service and overlay permissions solely to provide automated on-screen tapping at the user’s request. Payments are processed by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment details.

2. Permissions and How They Are Used

  • Accessibility Service: Used to perform automated taps and related actions that you initiate. No content on your screen is transmitted to us.
  • Overlay (“draw over other apps”): Used to display floating controls and indicators on top of other apps. No data is collected from overlays.

3. Data Collection

We do not collect analytics, device identifiers, or personal information within the app. If you contact us for support, we may receive your email address and any information you choose to provide, which we will use only to respond to your inquiry.

4. Payments

Access to the app’s functionality requires an active subscription, available on a monthly or yearly basis. All subscriptions and payments are handled by Google Play. Your billing relationship is with Google, and their privacy practices apply to payment processing. We do not store payment information.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” above and may provide notice in the app.
